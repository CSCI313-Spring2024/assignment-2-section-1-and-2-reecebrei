About Reece:

I am a sophomore at North Dakota State University (NDSU), where I am pursuing a degree in Software Engineering. My academic focus is centered on building a solid foundation in computer science and software development, with an emphasis on coding, problem-solving, and system design. I have developed a keen interest in learning various programming languages, algorithms, and the principles that drive innovative technology solutions. Alongside my studies, I work part-time at John Deere, where I contribute to real-world projects and gain hands-on experience in the field of engineering and technology. This role allows me to apply my academic knowledge in a professional environment while learning valuable skills in collaboration, problem-solving, and system optimization. It also exposes me to the cutting-edge technologies used in agriculture and manufacturing, aligning with my goal of working on impactful projects that combine engineering with technology. As I continue to progress in my studies and professional experiences, I am eager to further develop my technical skill set and explore opportunities that will allow me to make significant contributions to the software engineering field.

Outside of my academic and professional endeavors, I have a wide range of personal interests that keep me engaged and help me unwind. One of my passions is soccer, which I enjoy both playing and watching. Soccer teaches me valuable lessons in teamwork, strategy, and discipline, and it provides a great way to stay active while engaging in friendly competition with friends and peers. In addition to soccer, I am a fan of Texas Hold'em poker. I regularly play the game with friends, where I get to exercise strategic thinking, patience, and decision-making under pressure. It's a fun way to socialize and challenge myself mentally, honing my ability to assess risk and opportunity in dynamic situations. Another hobby I am passionate about is Magic: The Gathering, a collectible card game that I play with friends and fellow enthusiasts. Magic allows me to immerse myself in complex strategies and engage in both competitive and casual gameplay. I appreciate the creativity and the mental agility the game demands, and it provides a fun escape from the day-to-day while allowing me to connect with others who share similar interests. These hobbies, alongside my studies and part-time work, contribute to a balanced life where I am continually learning, connecting with others, and striving to improve both personally and professionally.
